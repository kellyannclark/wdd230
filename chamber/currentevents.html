Current Events

Winter Carnival

Come join us for the 57th annual McCall Winter Carnival from January 28th to February 6th. This year's theme is "Superheroes". There will be a variety of winter sports, live music, and plenty of food and drink to enjoy.

Ice Fishing Derby

Don't miss the 32nd annual Payette Lake Ice Fishing Derby on February 12th and 13th. There will be over $150,000 in cash and prizes, and all proceeds benefit local charities.

Spring Art Show

Join us for the McCall Art and Humanities Council's annual Spring Art Show, featuring the work of local artists. The show will run from March 12th to April 30th at the Central Idaho Historical Museum.

Farmers Market

The McCall Farmers Market opens for the season on May 21st. Come out to the Depot Park on Saturdays from 10am to 2pm to support local farmers and artisans and enjoy fresh produce, handmade crafts, and more.
